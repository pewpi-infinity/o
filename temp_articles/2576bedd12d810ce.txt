    <title>About spin electromagnetic wave-particle with ring singularity</title>
    <updated>2011-12-19T19:05:13Z</updated>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1112.4437v1" rel="alternate" type="text/html"/>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/1112.4437v1" rel="related" type="application/pdf" title="pdf"/>
    <summary>An axisymmetric space-localized solution of nonlinear electrodynamics is considered as massive charged particle with spin and magnetic moment. The appropriate solution for nonlinear electrodynamics with ring singularity is investigated. In view of this problem the system of toroidal waves in linear electrodynamics is considered. The problem with boundary conditions on the singular ring of the toroidal coordinate system is investigated. The boundary conditions are taken taking into account the conformity between the toroidal and cylindrical waves on the ring. In this case the singular ring looks like convolute axis of cylindrical system. The appropriate system of wave modes are obtained in an integral form with the help of source function.</summary>

    <title>About spin particle solution in Born-Infeld nonlinear electrodynamics</title>
    <updated>2009-11-17T08:14:54Z</updated>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/abs/0911.3230v1" rel="alternate" type="text/html"/>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/0911.3230v1" rel="related" type="application/pdf" title="pdf"/>
    <summary>  The axisymmetric static solution of Born-Infeld nonlinear electrodynamics with ring singularity is investigated. This solution is considered as a static part of massive charged particle with spin and magnetic moment. The method for obtaining the appropriate approximate solution is proposed. An approximate solution is found. The values of spin, mass, and magnetic moment is obtained for this approximate solution.</summary>

    <title>Electromagnetic wave-particle with spin and magnetic moment</title>
    <updated>2007-11-15T20:15:21Z</updated>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/abs/0711.2499v1" rel="alternate" type="text/html"/>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/0711.2499v1" rel="related" type="application/pdf" title="pdf"/>
    <summary>  An axisymmetric static solution of a nonlinear electrodynamics is considered as a massive charged particle with spin and magnetic moment. A linearization of the nonlinear electrodynamics around the static solution is investigated. The appropriate problem for linear waves around the static solution is considered. This wave part of the particle solution is considered to provide the appropriate wave properties for the particle. It has been found that the right (experimentally proved) formula for frequency of this wave appears theoretically for the static solution with ring singularity.</summary>
