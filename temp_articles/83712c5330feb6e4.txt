    <title>Neutrino photoproduction on pseudo Nambu-Goldstone bosons</title>
    <updated>2013-09-04T06:41:34Z</updated>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1207.1263v3" rel="alternate" type="text/html"/>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/1207.1263v3" rel="related" type="application/pdf" title="pdf"/>
    <summary>Production of single neutrinos as well as neutrino-antineutrino pairs by photons interacting with pseudo Nambu-Goldstone bosons is studied within the Standard Model. The corresponding cross sections are found analytically. The energy loss due to neutrino emission in a thermal plasma of photons and pions is calculated. It is shown that the obtained neutrino emissivity may be significantly enhanced in hot and dense matter due to in-medium modification of the pion decay constant. Phenomenological consequences for ultrarelativistic heavy-ion collisions and astrophysics are discussed.</summary>

    <title>Ultra-high energy neutrino dispersion in plasma and radiative transition $ν_L \to ν_R + γ$</title>
    <updated>2011-11-14T11:36:45Z</updated>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1108.5620v2" rel="alternate" type="text/html"/>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/1108.5620v2" rel="related" type="application/pdf" title="pdf"/>
    <summary>Qualitative analysis of additional energy of neutrino and antineutrino in plasma is performed. A general expression for the neutrino self-energy operator is obtained in the case of ultra-high energies when the local limit of the weak interaction is not valid. The neutrino and antineutrino additional energy in plasma is calculated using the dependence of the $W$ and $Z$--boson propagators on the momentum transferred. The kinematical region for the neutrino radiative transition (the so-called "neutrino spin light") is established for some important astrophysical cases. For high energy neutrino and antineutrino, dominating transition channels in plasma, $ν_e + e^+ \to W^+$, $\barν_e + e^- \to W^-$ and $\barν_{\ell} + ν_{\ell} \to Z$, are indicated.</summary>

    <title>Higher order correction to the neutrino self-energy in a medium and its astrophysical applications</title>
    <updated>2007-02-27T17:05:34Z</updated>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-ph/0702280v1" rel="alternate" type="text/html"/>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/hep-ph/0702280v1" rel="related" type="application/pdf" title="pdf"/>
    <summary>  We have calculated the 1/M^4 (M the vector boson mass) order correction to the neutrino self-energy in a medium. The possible application of this higher order contribution to the neutrino effective potential is considered in the context of the Early Universe hot plasma and of the cosmological Gamma Ray Burst fireball. We found that, depending on the medium parameters and on the neutrino properties (mixing angle and mass square difference) the resonant oscillation of active to active neutrinos is possible.</summary>
